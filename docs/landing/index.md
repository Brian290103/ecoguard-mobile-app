## 2. Key Features

A section with 3-4 key feature highlights, each with an icon, a short title, and a brief description.

*   **Feature 1: Effortless Reporting**
    *   **Description:** Quickly submit detailed reports of environmental issues with GPS location and media attachments (photos and videos).
*   **Feature 2: Real-Time Tracking**
    *   **Description:** Stay informed with real-time status updates on your reports. Know exactly when your report is reviewed, assigned, and resolved.
*   **Feature 3: Community Collaboration**
    *   **Description:** Connect with local environmental organizations and authorities dedicated to making a difference.
*   **Feature 4: News and Resources**
    *   **Description:** Access a curated feed of environmental news, events, and educational resources to stay engaged and informed.

## 3. How It Works

A simple, step-by-step visual guide explaining the core workflow. This can be a numbered list with icons or a simple infographic.

*   **Step 1: Report an Issue**
    *   A community member spots an environmental problem and submits a report via the EcoGuard app in minutes.
*   **Step 2: Officer Triage**
    *   An environmental officer reviews the report, verifies it, and assigns it to the most suitable local organization.
*   **Step 3: Organization Resolves**
    *   The assigned organization takes action to address and resolve the reported issue.
*   **Step 4: Get Notified**
    *   The user who submitted the report is notified once the issue has been marked as resolved.

## 4. Our Target Audience

A section dedicated to the different user roles within the EcoGuard ecosystem, explaining the benefits for each.

*   **For the Public:**
    *   Empowering you to be the eyes and ears of your community. Make a tangible impact on your local environment.
*   **For Environmental Organizations:**
    *   Streamline your workflow. Receive, manage, and track environmental reports assigned to you in one place.
*   **For Environmental Officers:**
    *   A powerful tool for oversight. Triage reports efficiently, delegate tasks, and monitor resolution progress across your jurisdiction.

## 6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

An accordion-style FAQ section to address common questions.

*   **Q1: Is EcoGuard free to use?**
    *   A: Yes, the EcoGuard app is completely free to download and use for all community members.
*   **Q2: What kind of issues can I report?**
    *   A: You can report a wide range of issues, including illegal dumping, pollution, blocked waterways, and damage to natural habitats.
*   **Q3: How do I know my report is being addressed?**
    *   A: You will receive real-time push notifications at every stage of the process, from submission to resolution.
*   **Q4: How can my organization join EcoGuard?**
    *   A: Organizations can apply to join the platform through our website. Once approved, you can start receiving and managing reports.
